Quantity Normalization Rules (STRICT)

- Quantity comes ONLY from AI output (quantity_raw)
- System must NEVER infer quantity from description
- Quantity must be numeric or numeric-decimal

Allowed examples:
- 10
- 12.5
- 1000

Rejected examples (flag for review):
- approx 10
- as required
- lot
- NIL
- one set

Rules:
- Remove commas (1,000 → 1000)
- Trim spaces
- If quantity cannot be parsed to number → mark LOW CONFIDENCE
- Do NOT delete item
- Do NOT guess quantity
