FAILURE & TRUST MESSAGING (MVP)

Purpose:
- Never blame AI
- Never say "error"
- Never say "failed"

Allowed System Messages:
- "Please review highlighted rows"
- "Some items need confirmation"
- "Low confidence detected"

Rules:
- AI output is always shown
- Ambiguity is handled via review, not blocking
- User is never forced to re-upload PDF

What NOT to show:
- Technical errors
- Model names
- Tokens / limits
- Confidence percentages

Tone:
- Calm
- Professional
- Engineer-friendly
